AI receipt scanning · exact-cent splits

Split the grocery bill, evenly.

You fronted the Costco run for the house. The pain isn't paying — it's the per-item math ("don't split the tomatoes for me"). Evenly scans the receipt, lets you toggle who's in per item, and computes everyone's exact share — to the cent.

Download for Android iOS — runs on-device via a dev build (no App Store)

The hard part isn't the total. It's who ate what.

Tap a person off an item and only their share changes — tax and fees redistribute proportionally, and the per-person totals always sum back to the receipt, exactly.
